About Us

If you are looking for an unforgettable experience in the Himalayas, look no further than Mountain Explorers. We are a Prague-based tour operator that specializes in trekking trips to the Himalayan region, in partnership with our local associates in Nepal. We have been operating since 2002, founded by a team of passionate mountaineers and adventurers. Our mission is to offer our clients a high-quality service that respects the environment and the culture of the Himalayan people. The Himalayas are the highest and most active mountain range in the world, with many of its peaks exceeding 8000 meters, including Mount Everest, the roof of the world. You will be amazed by the diversity and beauty of this region, from its rugged landscapes to its rich spiritual traditions. You will also discover why people say that "Heaven is myth, Himalaya is real." Whether you want a challenging expedition or a relaxing stroll, we have a trip for you. Contact us today and let us help you plan your dream Himalayan adventure.
